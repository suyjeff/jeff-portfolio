About Me

                    What started off as a random opportunity courtesy of a friend ended up being an amazing journey into the world of design, products, and amazingly talented people. Design has always felt like the place where "stuff happens", which keeps me really excited about all of the work I'm doing! Most of all, I've loved exploring design systems, the ability of design to empower people, and learning to see and build beautiful interfaces.

                    So far, I've designed web and mobile apps at Onfo, a cryptocurrency startup; led design and created a design system for Welfie; worked on the MuleSoft Anypoint Platform at Salesforce as a UX design intern; and had a wonderful experience as a product design intern at MongoDB in New York.

                    I'm currently a senior at Washington University in St. Louis, where I try to push my own limits and enjoy life! On the side, I love everything NBA, winning DotA 2 games, chewing gum, and eating chicken salad sandwiches everywhere they're sold.
